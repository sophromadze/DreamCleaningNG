Our Cleaning Services

Dream Cleaning offers a comprehensive range of professional cleaning services designed to meet all your residential and commercial needs across Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens. Whether you need regular maintenance or specialized deep cleaning, our experienced team delivers exceptional results every time.

What We Offer:

Residential Cleaning: From regular weekly or bi-weekly maintenance to thorough deep and super deep cleaning services, we keep your home spotless and healthy. Our eco-friendly products and systematic approach ensure every corner of your living space receives the attention it deserves.

Move In/Out Services: Starting fresh or leaving your old place? Our comprehensive move-in and move-out cleaning services ensure your space is pristine. We handle complete sanitization, cabinet cleaning, and detailed surface preparation to help you transition smoothly.

Specialized Cleaning: We tackle the toughest challenges including post-renovation cleanup, heavy-duty filthy conditions, and commercial spaces. Our team is equipped to handle construction debris, dust removal, and intensive sanitization for offices, retail spaces, and other commercial properties.
